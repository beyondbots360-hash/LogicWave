# Marketing & Digital Insights: Black Piano

## Content Strategy
- **The "Journal"**: Their blog is extremely robust. It targets high-intent keywords like "Cost to hire a Personal Assistant UK" or "Remote.com alternatives".
- **Topical Authority**: They cover legal nuances (IR35, EOR services in India cost) to capture traffic from cautious business owners.

## Conversion Funnel
1. **Top of Funnel**: High-value blog content and guides.
2. **Middle of Funnel**: "Schedule a Call" CTAs throughout the site, leading to a personal consultation.
3. **Bottom of Funnel**: Transparent pricing examples and case studies (e.g., Fabiano, CEO of Parabellum RE).

## Visual Identity
- **Branding**: Likely uses a high-contrast black/white/gold or premium palette to match the name "Black Piano".
- **Language**: Uses British English and specific UK business terminology (e.g., "Job Tax", "National Insurance").

## Technical SEO Observations
- **Keyword Targeting**: Aggressive targeting of "Hire [Role] in India for UK" and "Staff Augmentation".
- **Internal Linking**: Heavily interlinked blog posts to build siloed authority for specific roles (e.g., "Hire SEO Experts").
