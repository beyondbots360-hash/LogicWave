# Service & Role Breakdown: Black Piano

## Core Service Pillars
1. **Remote Recruitment**: Finding and vetting talent.
2. **Employer of Record (EOR)**: Handling payroll, taxes, and legal compliance in India.
3. **Remote HR**: Ongoing employee management and retention.

## Specialized Roles Offered
### 1. Technology & Data
- **Developers**: Android, iOS, Backend, DevOps, Front-end, Full stack, Java, Laravel, Python, React JS, React Native, Salesforce, Shopify, WordPress.
- **Data**: Data Analysts, Data Engineers.
- **Emerging Tech**: AI and Machine Learning Engineers.
- **Management**: Technical Architects, Scrum Masters, Project Managers.

### 2. Operations & Admin
- Virtual Assistants & Personal Assistants.
- HR Consultants & Recruiters.
- Business Operations & Delivery Management.

### 3. Creative & Marketing
- **Design**: UI/UX Designers, Graphic Designers, Illustrators.
- **Media**: Video Editors.
- **Marketing**: Content Writers, SEO Experts, Google Ads Experts.

### 4. Finance
- Accountants, Bookkeepers, Finance Managers.

## Pricing Structure (Inferred)
- **Model**: Monthly Cost = Employee Salary (Market rate) + Black Piano Management Fee.
- **Focus**: Emphasizes cost savings compared to UK in-house hiring (e.g., comparing their monthly cost to "Average UK cost").
